About us

We are a team from Pilot Program in Engineering (Software Engineering), Jilin University, concentrating on DSD course now. Our interested project is artificial intelligence algorithm for image processing of scoliosis.
During the previous study, some of our teammates have participated in research projects related to AI, and we believe that these shared experiences will help us reach a better achievement.
We are looking forward to fellow students to join us. Your wisdom and contribution will help this team accomplish its goals happily and well.. Feel free to contact us via email, or QQ group with the link as follows.
